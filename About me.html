My "3S"approach combined with blended learning

Systematic

Plan and formulate clear objectives that relate to measurable outcomes to progressively build on employee skills and allow the monitoring of performance so you get the results you want.

Storytelling

Why Storytelling? Research suggest that facts are twenty times more likely to be remembered if they’re part of a story. Learning facts devoid of context taxes the memory and without context what we learn soon fades. By creating learner focused training that build on critical thinking and perspective by using real world implication through the use of case studies, scenarios and storytelling creates a memorable, engaging and relatable learning experience which will lead to the drive in performance that your company wants.

Solutions

Assess performance and learner needs to identify gaps in knowledge and skills to develop specialised effective training that is customised to achieving the desired goals you are looking for.

Mission

To transform training and professional development into engaging and memorable learning experiences through the use of creative blended learning design and storytelling, so companies can form a thriving, progressive and healthy organisational culture.
